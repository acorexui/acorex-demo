Minimizable Modal This modal can be minimized to the bottom-right corner of the screen. Click the minimize button in the header to minimize this modal. It will appear as a small bar at the bottom of the screen.
Maximizable Modal This modal can be maximized to fullscreen mode. Click the maximize button in the header to enter fullscreen mode. Press Escape or click the restore button to exit fullscreen.
Full Control Modal This modal has both minimize and maximize controls. You can minimize it to the bottom-left corner or maximize it to fullscreen. Try both controls to see how they work together.
